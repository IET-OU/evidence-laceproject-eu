B: Teaching

Learning analytics improve learning support and teaching, including retention, completion and progression.

This proposition is about improvements to teaching and learning that are not direct learning gains by the learner.

This map links the evidence in the Evidence Hub with the location where it was gathered. Zoom in to the map, or click on one of the numbers for a more detailed view. Once you have done this, you can click on a coloured pin for more information, and then click through to the evidence.

Using the Search or Filter options will change the evidence that appears on the maps. Use the Search box to find evidence based on the words in its title. Use the Filter bar to search by Type (evidence of project), by Proposition (related to learning, teaching, uptake or ethics), by Polarity (positive, negative or neutral/mixed) and by Sector (schools, universities, workplace or informal).
